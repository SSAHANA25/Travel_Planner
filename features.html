Frequently Asked Questions

How many destinations can I add to a single trip?

You can add unlimited destinations to a single trip. Our platform is designed to handle complex itineraries with multiple stops, whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a multi-month world tour.

Can I share my travel plans with others?

Yes! TravelEase makes collaborative planning easy. You can share your itineraries with travel companions, who can view and edit plans (with your permission). This is perfect for group trips and family vacations.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your travel data. Your personal information and travel plans are secure, and we never share your data with third parties without your consent.

Does TravelEase work offline?

Yes, you can access your travel plans even without an internet connection. Simply download your itineraries before your trip, and you'll have access to all your important details during your travels.
