Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes! We offer a 15% discount when you choose annual billing instead of monthly billing. This applies to both Pro and Business plans.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for our Pro plan so you can experience all the premium features. No credit card is required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for Business plans, we also accept bank transfers.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, you'll still have access to your paid features until the end of your billing period.
